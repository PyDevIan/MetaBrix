## 2. Transition Plan ‚Äî MetaBrix Open Innovation Charter
As the community grows, MetaBrix will publish its **custom license** and **open innovation charter**, modeled after the *Business Source License (BSL 1.1)*.

### Draft Intentions:
- **Free for personal, educational, or research use.**
- **Commercial or hosted services** require explicit permission or a commercial license from MetaBrix.
- **Transparency and contribution** are encouraged under contributor agreements (to protect intellectual integrity).
- After a defined period (e.g. 3 years), code may transition to an **open license** such as Apache 2.0.

This system keeps the **ethics of open science** while ensuring MetaBrix can **sustain itself financially** and reward innovation.

## 4. Why This Approach
MetaBrix exists at the intersection of **science, AI, and hardware**.  
We must protect our ability to **fund long-term research** while keeping our technology **transparent and ethical**.

This license plan:
- Encourages collaboration with researchers and innovators.  
- Prevents corporate misuse or cloning during early growth.  
- Lays the groundwork for sustainable open science.
